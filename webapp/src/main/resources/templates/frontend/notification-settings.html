Notification Settings

You can receive notifications about current or upcoming releases at a frequency of 2 or 4 weeks. The notifications are always sent on Sundays. It is also possible to be notified on the release date or the day a new release is announced.

All you need to do is configuring your preferred channels.



Notifications can be sent per Telegram too! To activate this generate a registration ID and send it in your Telegram app to our bot 'metal_detector_notification_bot'.

Telegram notifications are activated for your account.
